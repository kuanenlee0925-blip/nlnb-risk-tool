Analysis Criteria

How NLNB risk is measured

The scoring model blends business continuity, operational resilience, technical exposure, vendor dependence, regulatory readiness, and modernization pressure into one risk profile.

Business Continuity

Measures transaction disruption, customer impact, revenue loss, and single-point-of-failure exposure.

Operational Stability

Looks at outages, incident response readiness, staffing, training, and backup effectiveness.

Technical Resilience

Evaluates legacy systems, patching, cybersecurity controls, cloud design, and system integration weaknesses.

Vendor Dependency

Scores third-party and fourth-party concentration, contract leverage, and external platform risk.

Regulatory Exposure

Reviews AML/KYC, FDIC, SEC, OCC, audit readiness, and data governance gaps.

Modernization Readiness

Assesses whether NLNB can support AI adoption, cloud migration, and future agentic banking initiatives safely.
